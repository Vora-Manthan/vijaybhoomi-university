About
The True School of Music

The True School of Music aims to nurture the next generation of professional musicians, music producers, and sound engineers. Our highly experienced faculty bring a global perspective and network to the learning experience. At True School of Music, we strive to provide our students with state-of-the-art equipment and technology

B.Mus (Hons.)
Music Production

This 4 year programme will prepare you for exciting careers in music, film, and the ever evolving digital entertainment industry. You will learn to compose, arrange, program and produce music for artists, films, ads, OTT content and games with guidance from faculty who are experienced industry professionals and exceptional mentors.

B.Mus (Hons.)
Music Production & Performance

This 4 year programme is a combination of the Music Production and the Contemporary Performance majors, crafted for students who are keen to build a versatile career that spans professional performance and original music production for a variety of media formats.

B.Mus (Hons.)
Contemporary Performance

Available Majors: Guitar, Bass, Drums, Piano, Western Vocals, Indian Vocals. This 4 year programme will introduce you to a broad range of performance technique exercises that develop a strong command over your instrument or voice, advanced harmonic and rhythmic concepts and performance as part of an ensemble.

B.Tech
Sound Engineering

This is a pioneering 4 year engineering program in India that focuses on all skill domains within sound engineering - Sound Recording & Engineering, Live Sound Engineering, Sound Design for Visuals, and Audio System Design and Development.
